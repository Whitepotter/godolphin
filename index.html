Daily life

Library

The school library is made up of 4 different rooms. 3 upstairs one downstairs. The rooms upstairs are the rooms where you have to be quiet while the one downstairs you can speak normally. The library includes a wide range of books and DVDs.

Sports hall

The largest hall in our school, we use the Sports hall for P.E. lessons,assemblies and much more. On one side of the hall is the full-size rock wall and on the other storage.

Auditorium

The Auditorium is the second largest hall in the school. We use it for whole school assemblies, Open days and most recently as a Covid testing area. We also use it for the many plays we do. Inside we have a stage and balconies for extra seating.

Awards

No 1 International Baccalaureate Diploma school in the UK for 2019

We are the No 1 International Baccalaureate Diploma school in the UK for 2019 and are equal second in the world – just pipped to top spot by a school in Singapore. Dr Frances Ramsey shared the news with members of staff extending ‘many congratulations to Ms Dubois, our IB Co-ordinator, and to all those who taught on the programme, helping the girls to achieve this world-beating success’.

Whats New

  • Covid-19 safety precautions

    The new safety precautions in our school are there to help us against Covid-19. That is why you must wear a mask while moving around in the school, take two LFD Covid-19 tests a week and also you must isolate if you have the virus or someone in your close family e.g. your mum, dad of sibling has it.
